The presence of the hinge support can be read as a reaction force \(\vec{R} \) whose direction is not known a priori, as in the case of the roller support. Therefore we will have the two reaction components, horizontal and vertical, possibly non-zero and independent

\begin{equation} H \neq 0 \,, \quad V \neq 0 \,,\quad \mathcal{M} = 0\,.\tag{5.3.7} \end{equation}
