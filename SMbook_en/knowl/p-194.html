\begin{equation} \func{\vec{t}}{\vec{x}, \vec{n}} =\func{\vec{t}}{\vec{x}, \vec{e}_1} \,n_1 + \func{\vec{t}}{\vec{x}, \vec{e}_2} \,n_2 + \func{\vec{t}}{\vec{x}, \vec{e}_3} \,n_3 \,,\tag{2.3.4} \end{equation}

relationship showing how traction \(\vec{t}\) is linked to the generic normal \(\vec{n}\) by means of a linear transformation, see video on linear transformations in SectionÂ 1.2, represented by a second order tensor. Then
