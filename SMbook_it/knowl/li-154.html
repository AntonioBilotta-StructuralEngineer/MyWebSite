Tratto \(CB\text{,}\) \(0 \leq x \leq l/2\text{:}\)
\begin{align*} N_{CB}(0) &= 0\,,\\ T_{CB}(0) &= T_{AC}(l/2) - F=F/2-F=-F/2\,,\\ M_{CB}(0) &= M_{AC}(l/2) = Fl/4\,. \end{align*}
Da cui si ottiene
\begin{align*} N_{CB}(x) &= 0\,,\\ T_{CB}(x) &= -F/2\,,\\ M_{CB}(x) &= -F/2\,x + Fl/4\,. \end{align*}
